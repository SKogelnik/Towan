Towan

Impressum

The website “Towan.us“ was created in the course of the lecture “Software Engineering Advanced“ as a student project under the instruction of the professors DI(FH)Johann Blauensteiner and Dipl. Ing. Mario Zupan. This course is part of the fourth semester of the bachelor degree programm “Information Managment“ at University of Applied Science in Graz.
“Towan.us“ is a tower defence game website that allows users to play a tower defence game with a personal account. This account keeps track of the users achievements that are represented by a statistic. This statistic shows the users improvements from one game to another.
